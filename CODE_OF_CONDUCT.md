# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

kube-router is maintained by a small team of volunteers. We welcome contributions from everyone and want this
to be a productive, respectful space.

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on the technical substance of discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully, and give it kindly
- Respect maintainer time --- come prepared, do your research, and keep discussions focused
- Credit others' work and ideas

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Spam, low-effort content, or disruptive behavior
- Any conduct that would be unwelcome in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and the
[#kube-router](https://kubernetes.slack.com/messages/C8DCQGTSB/) Slack channel.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at `admin@kube-router.io`. All reports
will be reviewed and may result in a response the maintainers deem appropriate, including warnings or permanent
bans from project spaces.
